Complete Workout Plan

Follow this structured workout plan to improve strength, endurance, and overall fitness.

Chest Workout

  • Bench Press – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 sets × 10 reps
  • Push-ups – 3 sets × max reps
  • Chest Fly – 3 sets × 12 reps

Rest: 60–90 seconds between sets

Leg Workout

  • Squats – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Leg Press – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • Walking Lunges – 3 sets × 20 steps
  • Calf Raises – 4 sets × 15 reps

Rest: 60–90 seconds between sets

Back Workout

  • Deadlifts – 4 sets × 6–8 reps
  • Pull-ups / Lat Pulldown – 3 sets × 10 reps
  • Seated Rows – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • Face Pulls – 3 sets × 15 reps

Rest: 60–90 seconds between sets

Weekly Schedule

  • Monday – Chest
  • Tuesday – Legs
  • Wednesday – Rest / Cardio
  • Thursday – Back
  • Friday – Cardio / Core
  • Saturday – Light Workout
  • Sunday – Rest

Tip: Always warm up before workouts and stretch after training to avoid injuries.
